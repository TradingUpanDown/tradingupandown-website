Questions about copy trading

What is copy trading?

Copy trading is a form of trading where traders can follow experienced strategy providers and replicate their positions.

It has benefits for both advanced traders and beginners, including saving time and diversifying your portfolio effortlessly. It also allows less experienced traders to enter global markets while leveraging on the extensive research and analysis of more seasoned traders.

Copy trading doesn't guarantee profits and involves the risk of losing your initial investment. You should always apply risk management tools to avoid significant losses.

For further information, please read our Copy Trading FAQs guide.

How do I open a provider or follower account?

Log in to your Client Portal to create an Equiti Copy account. Equiti Copy accounts are available for Standard and Premium clients. Please note that copy trading is only available on swap-enabled Equiti accounts.

If you don’t have an existing Equiti account, start by signing up and then apply for an Equiti Copy account.

Can I start copying trades on MT4, MT5 or Equiti Trader mobile app?

Copy trading is not available on MT4, MT5 or Equiti Trader mobile app. Open an Equiti Copy account and log in to the Equiti Copy platform to start copying trades.

Do I have to copy all of a provider’s trades?

No. You have the option to copy only specific direction (long/short) or trades within certain volume thresholds.

How do I stop following a provider?

You can decide to stop following a provider at any time with the following options:

Suspension: Stops all future copying but keeps your already open positions. Trades can be closed on the MetaTrader platform manually.

Unsubscribe: You can choose to keep or close your remaining trades.

What are the risk management tools available on Equiti Copy?

There are various risk management settings that will allow you to adjust the level of risk, including:

  • Minimum and maximum open lots
  • Maximum open volume
  • Total profit and loss max thresholds
